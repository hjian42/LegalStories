Overview of international rankings of the United States of America

The following are links to some international rankings of the United States.

Economics[edit]

  • World Economic Forum 2018–2019 Global Competitiveness Report, ranked 2nd out of 141 countries[1]
  • World Economic Forum 2016 Global Enabling Trade Report ranked 22nd[2]
  • The Heritage Foundation and The Wall Street Journal 2018 Index of Economic Freedom ranked 17th out of 178 economies[3]
  • Fraser Institute Economic Freedom of the World 2019 Annual Report (Economic Freedom Ratings for 2017) ranked 5th out of 152 countries and territories [2]
  • The Harvard Growth Lab ranked 12th in technological advancement, as of 2019.[4]

Education[edit]

  • Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development 2015 Programme for International Student Assessment, ranked 38 of 79 in mathematics, 19 of 79 in science, 13 of 78 in reading[5]

Globalization[edit]

Main article: Globalization
  • 2006 Globalization Index (A.T. Kearney) ranked 3rd
  • 2010 KOF Index of Globalization ranked 27th

Health[edit]

Main article: Healthcare in the United States

As of 2015[update], the Maternal mortality ratio was 46th lowest out of the countries ranked. (See Maternal mortality in the United States.)

Among wealthy nations, a study on 2016 data found the United States ranked first for child deaths by automobile accident and firearm, with overall child mortality 57% higher in the U.S. than other high-income countries, although traffic deaths were decreasing.[6]

As of 2017[update], the United States life expectancy is 79.8 years at birth, ranking 42nd among 224 nations.[7]

Peace[edit]

  • Vision of Humanity 2018 Global Peace Index ranked 121st out of 162 countries[8]

Politics[edit]

Main article: Politics of the United States
  • Transparency International 2019 Corruption Perceptions Index: ranked 23rd lowest out of 198 countries[9]
  • Reporters Without Borders 2022 Press Freedom Index: ranked 42nd out of 180 countries[10]
  • V-Dem Democracy indices: ranked 2023 27th out of 179 countries[11]
  • Democracy Index (The Economist): ranked 2022 30th out of 167 countries[12]
  • World Justice Project 2020 Rule of Law Index ranked 21st out of 128 countries and jurisdictions[13]

Standard of Living[edit]

  • List of countries by Human Development Index: ranked 13th out of 189 countries for 2018[14]
  • List of countries by inequality-adjusted HDI: ranked 25th out of 156 countries for 2018[15]
